My UX Skills

User Research

I let the question, “What problems are people trying to solve?” Guide my research. Performing over 50 user interviews and test, I have a wide range of user research experience.

User Empathy

Empathy maps, affinity diagrams, and user persons are just a few of the tools I use to understand the users and direct our problem solving.

Qualitative Interviews

To truly understand the user experience I focus on the “why.” Often using “The 5 Whys Metho.” Leading to deeper understanding of user behavior.

My Work

Travel App

I created this app to from ideation to high fidelity prototype. Designed to help the travel industry during the COVID19 pandemic it provides an easy way for travelers to access the most up-to-date information in order to stay safe and responsible.

Non-Profit Redesign

Working with a team, we worked with a local Salt Lake City non-profit to increase the usability of their site. With in-depth user research and stake holder interviews we developed creative solutions to merge the desires of both groups.

US Dept of Education

A real exercise in UI design and working with large amounts of data, I redesigned the US Department of Education’s site. Adjusting web flow and navigation based on card sorting, the new redesign is more user friendly.
